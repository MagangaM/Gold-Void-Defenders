# ⚔️ GOLD VOID DEFENDERS ⚔️

A retro-style space shooter built with pure **HTML5 Canvas**, featuring progressive difficulty and smooth gameplay.

![Game Preview](https://via.placeholder.com/800x400/0a0a0a/ffd700?text=GOLD+VOID+DEFENDERS)

## 🎮 Game Description

**Gold Void Defenders** is a modern take on classic arcade space shooters. Pilot a golden armored spaceship and defend against waves of increasingly aggressive aliens. 

The game features **progressive difficulty** — aliens get faster, more numerous, and fire more frequently as you advance through levels. Uniquely, when aliens reach your ship line, you automatically advance to the next level (instead of losing).

## ✨ Features

- **Progressive Difficulty System** — Levels get harder with more aliens, faster movement, and increased fire rate
- **Multiple Alien Types** with different point values:
  - **Gold Aliens** (50 pts) — High value, slower
  - **Orange Aliens** (30 pts)
  - **Red Aliens** (20 pts) — Fast and numerous
- **Smooth Controls** with responsive spaceship movement
- **Particle Explosions** and visual effects
- **Persistent Leaderboard** (saved in browser)
- **Lives System** (4 lives)
- **Endless Gameplay** — Levels continue indefinitely

## 🎯 How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in any modern browser
2. Click **"START MISSION"**
3. Use **Arrow Keys** or **A/D** to move
4. Press **SPACE** to fire repulsor blasts
5. Destroy aliens or survive until they reach your ship to advance levels

**Tip**: Let some aliens reach the bottom to progress faster while racking up points!

## 🕹️ Controls

| Key              | Action             |
|------------------|--------------------|
| ← → or A/D       | Move spaceship     |
